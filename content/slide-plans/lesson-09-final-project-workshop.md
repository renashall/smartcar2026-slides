## Slide 4 — Brainstorm Your Project

**On screen**
- Combine what you've learned into one idea. Starters:
  - **Obstacle dodger** — drive, and stop/turn when the ultrasonic sees something close.
  - **Light seeker** — use the two light sensors to steer toward the brighter side.
  - **Greeter bot** — face tracking + LEDs/buzzer when it spots a face.
  - **Status car** — LEDs change color with battery level (the watchdog idea).
- Pick something you can finish today — small and working beats big and broken.

## Coach Notes (hidden)

**Timing:** Slides 1–4 ~8–10 min total, then the **rest of class is work time** (slide 5 stays up). Give a time check at the halfway point and ~10 min before the end.

**Before class**
- Confirm every student can open their Pi, reach `Code/User`, and run code from earlier lessons.
- Cars charged; have spare chargers ready.
- Have the brainstorm starters (slide 4) handy for students who freeze on ideas.

**How to help**
- Guide and unblock — don't write their project for them. Ask leading questions.
- Push for **achievable scope**: one or two combined behaviors that actually run.
- Encourage frequent saving and testing in small steps.

**End of session**
- Make sure each student's project is saved and runnable.
- Remind everyone that next class is presentations.
- Post the class announcement / final-meeting reminder (template:
  https://aicode-coach-resources.notion.site/Final-Meeting-Announcement-9ff5b8cc4d9b4d8ba2691eb52f1b023e).
