## Slide 3 — Presentation Guidelines

**On screen**
- When it's your turn, tell us:
  1. **Your goal** — what did you set out to build?
  2. **What you worked on** — the parts you put together.
  3. **Any issues** — what was tricky, and how you dealt with it.
  4. **How your code works** — walk us through it (and demo it if you can!).
- Keep it short and just talk us through your project.

## Coach Notes (hidden)

**Timing:** Slides 1–3 ~5 min, then **most of class is presentations** (slide 3 stays up). Budget a few minutes per student; keep things moving so everyone gets a turn.

**Running presentations**
- Give students who need it a little extra build time first, if it fits.
- Lead the applause after each presentation; enforce the quiet-audience rule kindly.
- Prompt presenters through the four guideline points if they get stuck.
- Encourage a live demo when the project runs.

**Certificates & winners** (per AI Code Academy coach process)
