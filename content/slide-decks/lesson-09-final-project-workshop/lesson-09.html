How to help during the build

Don't present this slide. Guide and unblock — the project stays theirs.

1

Guide, don't write it for them

Ask leading questions and unblock — let the student keep ownership of the code.

2

Push for achievable scope

One or two combined behaviors that actually run — steer away from runaway ideas.

3

Save and test often

Encourage small steps: build a little, test, save, repeat. Reframe mistakes as data.

Coach mindset

Confidence-building over correctness. Celebrate every working step.
