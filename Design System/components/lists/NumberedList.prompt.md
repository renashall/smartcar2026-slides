Numbered checklist with green numbered markers — CheckList + StepList combined. Use for ordered lesson goals.

```jsx
<NumberedList items={[
  "Connect to the Raspberry Pi over Wi-Fi",
  "Run your first lesson script",
  "Drive the car around an obstacle on its own",
]} />
```

Items: plain strings or `{ title, body }`. `start` sets the first number; `size` = sm / md / lg. Green numbered circles, hairline dividers.
