Image cards. MaterialCard (product image + name + count subtitle, for "what's in the kit" grids), ImageFrame (large plain image with no title or padding, for hero shots and diagrams), ScreenshotCard (a wide window/display capture with a step badge and a 1–2 line caption along the bottom), and VideoCard (a clickable poster with a play overlay and a bottom-left subtitle linking to a video). Size each from its parent; the image fills the frame.
