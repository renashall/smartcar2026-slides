Compact pill label for status, lesson numbers and category tags.

```jsx
<Badge tone="teal">Beginner</Badge>
<Badge tone="amber">New</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" icon={<img src="assets/icons/success.png" width="12" />}>Passed</Badge>
<Badge tone="solid">Lesson 4</Badge>
```

Tones: `navy`, `teal`, `amber`, `neutral` (default), `success`, `warning`, `error`, `solid` (filled navy). Optional `icon` slot renders before the label.
